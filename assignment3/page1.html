Tylenol Appetizer

Created by Chef Lucella, this dish was inspired by the everyday pains we go through and how medicine plays such a huge role in our lives. Tylenol allows us to function when we're low on energy, aching, or general pains. This appetizer reminds us to embrace pain, for it is only temporary.
